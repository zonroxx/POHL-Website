About the Corporation

About the Corporation

Vision Statement

POHL provides legal, referral and support services to indigent nationals and aliens within the Pacific Region, which includes U.S. Territories, Freely Associated States, and Island Nations, who are victims of Sexual Assault, Domestic Violence, Dating Violence, Stalking, and Human Trafficking including victims of immigration abuse, fraud in foreign labor contracting, labor abuse, debt bondage, peonage, employment discrimination and criminal activities.

And through the development of best practices guidelines and policies, the larger goal is to increase the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands' awareness and approach to eliminating crimes of Sexual Assault, Domestic Violence, Dating Violence, Stalking, and Human Trafficking including victims of immigration abuse, fraud in foreign labor contracting, labor abuse, debt bondage, peonage, employment discrimination and criminal activities facing the CNMI and Pacific Region.

Mission Statement

POHL is recognized as providing legal, referral and support services for victims of all crimes in the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands. POHL continually collaborates with social network providers, law enforcement agencies and advocate services in order to secure the most effective and expeditious relief available for victims under federal and local laws.

POHL serves victims with the express intent to rescue victims and reintegrate survivors back into the community with hope for a more sustainable future. This mission will be implemented first in the CNMI through the Humanitarian Opportunities for Positive Empowerment project (H.O.P.E.). H.O.P.E. Project supports all victims, especially those of Sexual Assault, Domestic Violence, Dating Violence, Stalking, and Human Trafficking.

The project includes identification; assessment; referral and reintegration process services; legal counseling and services; language access support, referral for legal representation whenever applicable; referral for emergency care and counseling services; referral to shelter and protection; and subsequent registration for training and certification programs in support of survivors' reintegration and future sustainability.

The Market

POHL currently provides legal services to victims of crimes within the CNMI, with plans for expansion into the Pacific Region. Legal services focus on aiding victims of domestic violence, sexual assault, including victims of sex trafficking, labor abuse, immigration abuse, trafficking in persons, discrimination and other criminal laws.

Operations

Our operations are centrally located with welcoming offices and hours of operation that accommodate all types of clientele. Our friendly multi-national staff is able to meet the needs of our diverse clientele.

Refugee Protection

POHL is committed to providing comprehensive support and legal assistance to refugees in the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, ensuring they receive the protection and resources they need to rebuild their lives in safety and dignity.
